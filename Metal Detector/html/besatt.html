Besatt

Arvid "Holtergeist" and Erik "Bodysnatcher" West are Besatt.
They describe themselves as a "A gory villain spreading fear, horror and dreads to the sleepy town of Grömstad, Norway”. It is certainly not easy for new bands to consistently come with new music over the years, but Besatt makes it seem simple. The underground duo are three albums and three singles deep already, and with such a vast catalogue early on they allow for new fans to discover and dive into their avant-garde black-metal world completely, and we believe it's worth it.

History

BESATT is a metal duo from Norway consisting of Arvid Holter and Leif Erik Westergren. They began seriously releasing music under the name Besatt in 2021. They have been independant, but have started releasing music with the help of the underground distribution label Narcoleptica Productions on primarily bandcamp. They also have their music on streaming platforms such as spotify. Narcoleptica Productions began distributing their music on May 27th 2022 with their first album DYST, which came digitally and on CD. This trend has continued with posting singles on bandcamp and having Narcoleptica Productions release their albums. They also sell CD and merchandise on ebay.



Their music has made it possible for them to have albums be featured on the Discogs Magazine page. Discogs Digs is a magazine for music discovery. Unlike us in Metal Detector, Discogs puts a spotlight on artists and bands of all kinds of genres. It's a page including both highly popular artists, however aren't afraid to include artist with younger careers. Great opportunity for Besatt and such a milestone for such a young band. Already in their second year getting valuable exposure and co-sign from not only underground labels and fans, but established pages like Discogs. In Metal Detector we like to highlight bands that exude potential, a yearning for creating music and Besatt is absolutely a band worth following as they grow their signature avant-garde black-metal style in the underground scene of metal music.
