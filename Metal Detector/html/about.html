Metal Detector is a minimalistic magazine dedicated solely to sharing the up and coming musical wonders of the Metal Genre
New bands in particular are often not given a platform and we see it as a privilege to be able to share the beauty of the blooming flowers hidden by a forest of trees with already deep roots and heights, hard to look past. It's as much the blooming flower as the foliating tree that makes the grand view a breathtaking one.


Check out our earlier highlights or follow us elsewhere to get the latest updates on new arrivals.

Our Mission

We in Metal Detector love to find and present new talent to build on the history of metal as a whole. This time around we've taken the trip to Norway. We want to highlight a select few bands that we feel showcases the genre, innovates with their style and creativity, and of course bands we feel can inspire both old and new fans of this undoubtedly classic genre. With great artist popping up all around the world it certainly seems to us that we have a responsibility to help spread the infectious and raw emotion that metal is with the world further. Music has the ability to affect us collectively and inspire us to do great things and to connect with us personally to understand ourselves better or to just let us release and be free. Who are we to withold such an experience?
